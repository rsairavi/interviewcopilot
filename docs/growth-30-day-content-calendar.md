# InfinityHire Copilot 30-Day Content Calendar

## Goal

Drive high-intent traffic to `https://infinityhire.ai`, improve signup conversion, and convert activated free users to Pro.

## Channels and cadence

- LinkedIn founder posts: daily
- Short-form video (YouTube Shorts/Reels): 3 per week
- Community distribution (Reddit/Discord/X): 4 per week
- Email nurture (new signups + trial users): 1 per week

## Content pillars

1. Live interview performance (confidence, structure, speed)
2. Role-specific prep (ML, data, backend, product)
3. Behind-the-scenes product build and user wins
4. Conversion assets (pricing proof, feature explainers, demo clips)

## 30-day plan

### Week 1: Problem awareness and authority

- Day 1: Founder story post: "Why interviews still fail smart candidates"
- Day 2: Demo clip: live question -> resume-aware answer in <3 seconds
- Day 3: Carousel: 5 mistakes in technical interviews and fixes
- Day 4: Community post with practical checklist for interview setup
- Day 5: Case-style post: before/after answer quality using STAR format
- Day 6: Short video: "How to avoid rambling in behavioral rounds"
- Day 7: Weekly recap + CTA to `Start Free`

### Week 2: Product education and trust

- Day 8: Feature post: role-specific modes for ML/data/backend
- Day 9: Objection handling: "Is this only for beginners?"
- Day 10: Demo clip: resume upload -> context-aware response quality
- Day 11: Community AMA prompt about live interview anxiety
- Day 12: Pricing explainer: why India-first pricing matters
- Day 13: Integration post: Zoom/Meet/Teams workflow in one tab
- Day 14: Weekly roundup with "Book Demo" CTA for teams

### Week 3: Activation and conversion push

- Day 15: "First 5 minutes" onboarding walkthrough
- Day 16: User-style scenario: "Data scientist round with real prompts"
- Day 17: Quota pressure post: when it makes sense to upgrade to Pro
- Day 18: Community tip thread: fast answer frameworks
- Day 19: Demo clip: session history and post-interview review
- Day 20: Feature deep dive: STAR answer formatting
- Day 21: Weekly recap + direct `Upgrade to Pro` CTA

### Week 4: Social proof, retention, and scale

- Day 22: Win post template: "Interview confidence improved by X"
- Day 23: Team use case: structured interview decisions with consistency
- Day 24: Demo clip: dashboard metrics and activation milestones
- Day 25: Community post: best follow-up questions interviewers ask
- Day 26: Comparison post: generic AI chat vs interview copilot workflow
- Day 27: Founder build log: what shipped this month and why
- Day 28: Case-style conversion post with CTA to Book Demo
- Day 29: Live Q&A session announcement
- Day 30: Month-end retrospective and next-month waitlist/launch hook

## CTA mapping

- Top of funnel posts: `Start Free`
- Mid funnel educational posts: `Try your first session`
- Bottom funnel posts: `Upgrade to Pro` and `Book Demo`

## Weekly scorecard

- Traffic to landing page
- Landing -> signup conversion
- Signup -> first question asked activation rate
- Free -> Pro conversion rate
- Cost per qualified signup (if running paid experiments)
