## One-liner

InfinityHire Copilot helps candidates answer better in live interviews with real-time, role- and company-specific AI guidance.

## Problem

Candidates often fail interviews due to pressure, communication, and delivery quality, not lack of technical skill.  
Most existing prep tools are generic and offline; they do not support the actual live interview workflow with role and company context.

## Solution

InfinityHire Copilot provides an interview-native AI workflow:

- real-time interview answers
- one-click best-answer rewrites
- company-specific question banks
- post-session debrief and coaching insights
- 7-day personalized prep plan
- shareable report and team panel summary

## Why now

- LLM quality and latency are now good enough for interactive coaching.
- Interview processes are becoming more structured and more competitive.
- Users already use AI, but mostly in generic chat interfaces that are not interview-optimized.

5. Generates debrief and 7-day prep plan.
6. Shares report or uses team panel summary.

## 2-Minute Pitch Script (draft)

InfinityHire Copilot is an AI copilot for live interview performance.  
The core problem is that candidates fail interviews because of delivery quality under pressure, not because they lack knowledge. Existing prep products are generic and disconnected from the real interview moment.

We built a workflow-native product. Users start a session, pick role and company mode, ask questions, and get interview-ready answers in seconds. Then they can rewrite answers, generate a debrief, and get a 7-day personalized prep plan.  
For teams, we provide panel summary workflows and shareable reports.

Our pricing is simple: free tier for activation, INR 499/month Pro for unlimited usage and advanced coaching, plus a Team tier for collaborative evaluation use cases.

We believe this can become the default AI layer for interview performance and hiring intelligence: starting with candidates, then scaling to team-level hiring decisions.
